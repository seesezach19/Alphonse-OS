# Bound diagnostic repair autonomy

Diagnostic Agents may autonomously detect failures, correlate traces, open cases, reproduce failures, form hypotheses, and commission bounded Repair Workers and targeted verification. Source writes and sensitive access are policy-gated, while merge, deployment, optional Behavior Contract changes, authority expansion, and acceptance of unresolved risk require humans initially; automatic promotion may only follow later through explicit proven repair-class policy.
