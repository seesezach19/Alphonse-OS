## Included Scope

- one dedicated customer-controlled single-tenant Alphonse installation;
- one selected existing n8n workflow;
- onboarding and exact accountable-coverage review;
- bounded incident diagnosis and inactive repair candidate creation;
- independent verification, named-Owner promotion, reconciliation/rollback, and assurance export;
- documented operator coordination during the pilot.

Hosted multi-tenancy, Kubernetes, SSO, compliance certification, 24/7 support, guaranteed uptime/response time,
greenfield workflow construction, and additional workflows are excluded unless separately agreed.

## Precommitted Success And Stop Conditions

Success requires all of the following, without waiver:

- an unfamiliar human operator completes the public-only journey with zero undocumented steps;
- at least one real incident is recorded against the selected real client workflow;
- at least one exact inactive repair is independently verified and authorized by the named Owner;
- target state is confirmed or safely reconciled/rolled back with zero duplicate consequential effects;
- the client reviews the exact assurance bundle, acknowledges limitations, and finds it useful;
- the agency records one of the two pre-agreed decisions within ten days: `retain_paid` or `decline`.

Stop and fence new work on credential exposure, scope drift, unbounded personal data, failed backup/restore, lost
target identity, revoked authority, unresolved target mismatch, duplicate effect, managed-TLS failure, or any action
requiring a prohibited system/class. Reconciliation and separately authorized recovery remain available where safe.

## Escalation Boundary

- Operator may inspect, suspend workers, quarantine the workflow, collect documented evidence, and request Owner
  action through exact public operations.
- Only the named Owner may approve/release/promotion/recovery actions reserved to Owner.
- Repair Worker cannot verify or promote; verifier cannot promote; support cannot receive standing customer authority.
- Ambiguous application is reconciled read-only before retry. Target mismatch requires human review. Rollback is a
  separate authorization and is never inferred from failure.
- Security, client-consent, or data-boundary incidents immediately end the pilot until the client and agency approve a
  documented recovery plan.

## Pricing Hypothesis And Decision

Internal hypothesis to validate, not a sent quote:

- onboarding: USD 2,500 once;
- retained service: USD 1,500/month for one installation and one workflow;
- additional workflows, response-time commitments, hosting, or bespoke integration are out of scope and separately
  priced.

Before the pilot begins, the agency acknowledges these hypothesis terms and agrees to decide within ten days after
the completed pilot: retain at or above USD 1,500/month (`retain_paid`) or decline (`decline`). "Interested," "extend
the free pilot," and "revisit later" are not passing payment decisions.
