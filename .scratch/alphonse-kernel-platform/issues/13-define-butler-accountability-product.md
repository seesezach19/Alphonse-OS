# Define Butler Accountability Product

Type: prototype
Status: resolved
Claimed by: Codex
Blocked by: 04, 05

## Question

What generic projections, alerts, explanations, queues, corrective Work Intent proposals, escalation routing, and recovery coordination should Butler provide over Kernel accountability records without becoming an authority source, privileged command path, or overwhelming operator task list?

Prototype asset: [Butler Accountability Product](../prototypes/butler-accountability-product.md)

## Answer

Butler is the minimum first-party accountability surface over Kernel: exception-focused inbox, Accountable Work Threads, separate execution/accountability status, evidence and Obligation views, exact action cards, Recovery Cases, structured runtime handoff, comments, and modular projections.

Mandatory work items require Kernel-backed approvals, Obligations, Escalations, Recovery Cases, handoffs, or policy exceptions. Butler intelligence separates fact, inference, recommendation, and uncertainty; it may prepare and propose but never approve, activate, satisfy obligations, or use a privileged recovery path. Butler V0 proves Slack for accountable agentic operations without attempting general messaging-platform parity.
