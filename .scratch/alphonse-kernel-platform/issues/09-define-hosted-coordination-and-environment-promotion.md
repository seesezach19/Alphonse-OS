# Define Hosted Coordination And Environment Promotion

Type: prototype
Status: resolved
Claimed by: Codex
Blocked by: 03, 08

## Question

What package promotion, update discovery, environment registration, health visibility, and support coordination may a hosted service provide while customer Kernel Environments retain exclusive authority over identity, activation, runs, evidence, and recovery?

Prototype asset: [Hosted Coordination And Environment Promotion](../prototypes/hosted-coordination-and-environment-promotion.md)

## Answer

Hosted Alphonse is a replaceable coordination plane, not an operational authority. Customer Environments initiate signed outbound connections and expose minimal Environment Descriptors, sanitized health, and receipt-derived promotion status. Hosted service distributes catalogs, advisories, and Promotion Proposals; each target Kernel independently resolves, reviews, deploys, and activates exact Packages under local policy.

A customer-defined Promotion Graph creates a clear development-to-staging-to-production path with signed evidence gates. Support requires temporary locally issued Support Passports, and hosted outage or termination cannot stop existing customer operations. Local, customer-cloud, and managed deployments use the same protocol; infrastructure custody is disclosed separately from Kernel authority.
